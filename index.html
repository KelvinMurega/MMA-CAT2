Why Wildlife Matters

Wildlife is essential to healthy ecosystems. Animals, plants, forests, rivers, and oceans all work together to support life on Earth. Unfortunately, habitat destruction, climate change, and illegal poaching continue to threaten many species. By protecting wildlife today, we preserve biodiversity, strengthen ecosystems, and secure a healthier planet for generations to come.
